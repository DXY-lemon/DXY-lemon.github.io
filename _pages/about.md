Xueying Du is a PhD Student at Fudan University, China. As a member of [CodeWisdom Group](http://www.se.fudan.edu.cn), she had the invaluable opportunity to work under the guidance and mentorship of [Prof. Xin Peng](https://cspengxin.github.io) and [Prof. Yiling Lou](https://yilinglou.github.io/index.html). Her research focuses on the intersection of Software Engineering (SE) and Artificial Intelligence (AI), specifically in the domains of AI4SE and SE4AI. Her primary interest lies in leveraging advanced AI technologies, such as large language models (LLMs) and knowledge graphs (KGs), to address software engineering challenges. Previously, he obtained his B.S. degree in Computer Science from Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2021.
